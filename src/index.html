Who we are and what we offer?

Thank you for visiting us! We believe you’re here for a reason. At our store, you’ll find more than just plants—you’ll find green companions that will become a part of your life.

  • Premium Quality Plants: Carefully selected from trusted suppliers.
  • Care Guidance: Detailed instructions to keep your plants thriving.
  • Eco-Friendly Delivery: Convenient and sustainable, right to your doorstep.

Why Choose Us?

  • Personalized Recommendations: We understand that every customer is unique, just like every plant. Our experts will help you choose plants that fit perfectly into your space and lifestyle.
  • Quality Assurance: Every plant is inspected to ensure it brings joy for years to come.
  • Sustainability: We care for the planet: recycled pots, eco-friendly packaging, and mindful practices.
  • Post-Purchase Support: Have questions? We're here to guide you every step of the way.

Find your perfect plant!

Not sure what to pick? We’re here to help!

  • For Your Home: Transform any room into a cozy oasis with decorative plants like Ficus, Monstera, or Peace Lily.
  • For Your Office: Looking for something low-maintenance? ZZ Plant and Snake Plant make great green accents for workspaces.
  • For Your Garden: Elevate your outdoor space with vibrant shrubs, trees, and flowering plants.

Need help deciding? Our Plant Selector Tool can recommend the best plants based on light, humidity, and care preferences.

Why Plants Are Special?

Every plant has its charm. Did you know?

  • Monstera can purify air by filtering harmful substances.
  • Succulents are perfect for busy people, as they thrive on minimal care.
  • Some plants, like Lavender and Aloe Vera, even have medicinal properties.

With each purchase, you’ll receive care instructions to ensure your green friends thrive.

The Story of Our Shop

Once upon a time, we were just plant enthusiasts creating cozy corners in our homes. Today, Planto. is a team of nature lovers eager to share our passion for greenery. We started with a small collection of succulents and grew into a store offering hundreds of plant varieties. We’re proud that our mission inspires thousands of people
